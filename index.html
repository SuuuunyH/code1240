Design concept
With the development of modern cities, people's demand for housing is getting higher and higher. Market demand determines the development trend of the housing industry. There are many differences in society. Of course, housing demand is different, and residential development with diversity and appropriate personality is needed. With the rapid development of the modern film industry, film has become one of the important occasions for people to relax, entertain and consume. So I think it would be nice to put these two together.
The cartoon of the design
The whole building is divided into two floors, the first floor is the cinema and the second floor is the house.
A qualified building should first consider safety issues, I set up a large area of space around the cinema, if the fire and other security problems happened we can effectively and quickly escape. Usually this place can also be used to queue tickets, will not be crowded
The first question to consider when combining cinemas and homes is the noise problem.
My solution to this is to wrap the entire house with soundproofing and noise-reducing wooden materials, minimizing the impact of noise on the residents
This is an elevator that is convenient for residents to go up and down the building, but for security reasons, the card can be used.
